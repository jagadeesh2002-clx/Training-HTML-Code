The Solar System

Introduction

The solar system consists of the Sun and everything bound to it by gravity — the planets, moons, asteroids, comets, and more.

Planets

1. Mercury

Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun and the smallest in our solar system.

2. Venus

Venus is known for its thick, toxic atmosphere and surface temperatures hot enough to melt lead.

3. Earth

Our home planet and the only one known to support life.

4. Mars

Often called the Red Planet, Mars is a focus of exploration for future human missions.

Interesting Facts

  • The Sun makes up 99.86% of the mass in the solar system.
  • Jupiter is the largest planet in the solar system.
  • Saturn's rings are made mostly of ice particles.

Conclusion

The solar system is a vast and fascinating place, with many mysteries still to explore.
